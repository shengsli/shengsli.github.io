22 Carbohydrate cycling

22.1 Basal metabolic rate (BMR) formula

  1. Harris-Benedict equation 1919:
    • For men, 13.7516*weight + 5.0033*height - 6.7550*age + 66.4730 kcal/day
  2. Revised Harris-Benedict equation 1984:
    • For men, 13.397 *weight + 4.799 *height - 5.677 *age + 88.362 kcal/day
  • 1.6 ratio
  • 1-1.2 g/lbs protein * weight * (1-bfp)
  • 1.25 g/lbs carbs * weight
