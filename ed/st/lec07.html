2 Structural testing

2.1 Why structural testing?

  • Specification might bot be complete.
  • Specification does not specify the implementation.
  • ensure no part of program is not tested.
  • structural elements: e.g. loops

2.2 No guarantees

  • execute all control flow elements does not guarantee finding all faults.

2.3 in practice

  • automated mean to test
